About Our School

Founded in 1923, Evergreen Academy began as a small, one-room schoolhouse in the heart of the community. The original goal was to provide quality education to local children, and over the decades, the school has grown significantly. In the 1950s, Evergreen Academy expanded its facilities to include additional classrooms and a library, reflecting its commitment to providing a broader range of educational resources. By the 1970s, the school had become a central part of the community, known for its progressive teaching methods and strong academic performance. The 1990s brought further modernization with the introduction of computer labs and advanced science laboratories. The new millennium saw the addition of a state-of-the-art auditorium and sports complex, underscoring the school's dedication to nurturing both academic and extracurricular talents. Today, Evergreen Academy serves over 1,200 students and is recognized for its innovative programs and strong emphasis on community involvement. As we look to the future, the school remains dedicated to evolving with educational trends while maintaining its rich tradition of excellence.
